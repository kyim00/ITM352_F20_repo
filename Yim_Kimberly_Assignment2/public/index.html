Welcome to Jewels By Kim!

We are a Hawaii based jewelry store specializing in handcrafted, ready-to-wear gold and silver plated earrings. Our mission is to make our jewelry affordable without compromising on quality. Jewels By Kim uses plated jewelry that is coated with real 14K gold and silver to produce our pieces. Our jewelry lasts for a lifetime and are elegant pieces that are perfect for every occasion. They make perfect gifts or treats for yourself! There are various designs that you can choose from and we will do our best to get it to you as quickly as possible!
